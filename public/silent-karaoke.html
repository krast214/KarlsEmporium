Silent Karaoke Rules

  • Player puts on headphones/earphones to start.
  • A random song will be shown to them and they have to sing along. 2 skips per round.
  • Sing for 1 minute. Others guess song and artist. 1 point each.
  • Teams: Priority to guess, then open to others. Free for all: 10s wait after wrong guess.
  • No lyrics for singer. Guesses are typed and submitted.
  • Round ends when both answers are correct or time runs out.
  • Scores shown at end of all rounds.
